Equipment

To own a ball python, you need to get a variety of equipment.

Some things you need are a tank (that is 1/2 to 2x the length of the ball python), a heat lamp, a water bowl (for bathing and drinking), hides (to keep the snake cool), shady items like logs or leaves, and bedding with either bugs or replacements.

This is my ball python's enclosure.

If you get all of these things, you can safely own a ball python and not hurt it.
